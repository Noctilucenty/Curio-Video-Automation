# CURIOSITY-TO-CURIO CONVERSION SYSTEM v1

> **LIVING DOCUMENT — ADOPTED 2026-07-19** (Codex directive, Leon approved).
> Read alongside `GROWTH_OS.md`. This governs how a Reel converts curiosity into Curio
> users **without** turning the Reel into something viewers recognise as an ad.

## Objective

Videos are not merely content for views. Their business purpose is to convert curiosity
into: **brand recognition → profile visits → link clicks → app installs → returning
users.**

**The advertisement must never weaken the hook, delay the factual payoff, damage the
loop, or make the Reel feel like a conventional ad.**

## THE ARCHITECTURE (Leon's strongest recommendation)

**Complete story in the Reel → "Go deeper with Curio" as a soft signature →
conversion CTA in the caption → direct Curio link in profile and Story.**

## NEVER WITHHOLD THE PAYOFF — non-negotiable

Every Reel must be satisfying by itself. **Never** structure a video as
*"Open Curio to learn what happened."* That damages trust, shares, saves, completion
and long-term brand value. Give the complete fact or story. **Curio promises the NEXT
layer of discovery — it never holds the current ending hostage.**

## Brand position

Curio should feel like where viewers go after a Reel makes them think:
> "Wait — what else don't I know?"

The video earns attention · the factual payoff earns trust · the Curio signature creates
memory · the caption and profile convert interest.

## TAGLINE SYSTEM

**Primary (default in-video signature): "GO DEEPER WITH CURIO"**
Four words · active · instantly understood · premium not clickbait · works across
science/psychology/history/survival · needs no explanation of "rabbit hole" · renders
visually without narration.

**Caption line (longer, more personality): "More rabbit holes like this live in Curio."**
Too long and passive for a repeated on-video outro — captions only.

Ranked alternatives: 2. THE RABBIT HOLE CONTINUES IN CURIO · 3. ONE RABBIT HOLE.
THOUSANDS MORE IN CURIO. *(only if "thousands" is literally accurate)* · 4. YOUR NEXT
RABBIT HOLE IS IN CURIO · 5. CURIOSITY GOES DEEPER IN CURIO.

**BANNED:** "Did you know…" · "Follow for more" · "Like and subscribe" · "Download now" ·
"You won't believe…" · "Wait until the end" · full-screen app ads · static logo cards ·
the same spoken sales sentence in every narration.

## IN-VIDEO SIGNATURE — specification

**Do NOT append a traditional outro.** Integrate a brief signature OVER footage already
part of the story.

- Copy: **GO DEEPER WITH CURIO**
- Duration: **0.6–0.9s**
- **Must not extend runtime** · must not replace the payoff · must not cover captions or
  the central subject
- Size: **~60–70% of normal caption size**
- Established clean white/cream brand typography · **no coloured box** · no app-store
  badge · no large logo animation · no musical sting · no spoken CTA by default
- **Clear the signature before the final loop-reset frames**; leave ~**0.20–0.30s**
  visually clean before the endpoint where needed for a seamless loop

**Sequence:** hook → evidence → explanation → payoff → brief Curio signature → clean
loop reset.
**NOT:** hook → explanation → advertisement → logo card → dead air.

**If a video has no safe place for the signature without damaging its ending, OMIT the
in-video CTA** and use caption + pinned comment + Story. **BRINE-POOL v7 is exactly such
a case — omission is correct there** (its "And the impossible part?" → "The ocean has
lakes." loop would be interrupted by any outro, likely reducing replays).

## Visual options for future testing

- **A — Minimal wordmark.** Small "CURIO" in an uncluttered upper third, final 0.7s.
  Best for strong semantic loops, dense final captions, busy frames.
- **B — Active signature (RECOMMENDED DEFAULT).** "GO DEEPER WITH CURIO", 0.7–0.9s over
  continuing footage, clears before the endpoint.
- **C — Contextual signature.** Topic line + stable brand line, e.g.
  "THE OCEAN GETS STRANGER." / "GO DEEPER WITH CURIO." Only with sufficient empty space;
  never more than two short lines.
- **D — Spoken CTA.** e.g. "The rabbit hole goes deeper in Curio." **Only ~1 in 4–5
  videos**, only when it sounds like a natural final thought. Never the default — spoken
  advertising adds runtime and can trigger early swipes.

## Repetition without fatigue

Keep the central phrase **constant**: "GO DEEPER WITH CURIO". Rotate only the contextual
lead: *The ocean gets stranger · One mystery down · There is always another layer · The
story continues · Follow the impossible · Your next rabbit hole is waiting.*
**Do not rotate the Curio name or core promise** — that prevents memory association.

## Profile conversion requirements

The Reel CTA fails if the profile does not immediately explain Curio.

> Daily stories that turn curiosity into rabbit holes.
> Go deeper in the Curio app ↓

Bio link lands on the app page, a fast mobile landing page, or ideally the specific topic
/ related collection. **Never a generic homepage with no obvious next action.**

**Curio destination: `https://trycurio.app`**

Per-Reel tracking — **separate source/medium per platform, never one shared link**:
| platform | link |
|---|---|
| Instagram | `https://trycurio.app/?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=reel&utm_campaign=<topic>` |
| Facebook | `https://trycurio.app/?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=reel&utm_campaign=<topic>` |
| YouTube | `https://trycurio.app/?utm_source=youtube&utm_medium=shorts&utm_campaign=<topic>` |
| TikTok | `https://trycurio.app/?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=video&utm_campaign=<topic>` |

**Instagram and Facebook get SEPARATE publishing blocks.** Instagram may say "link in
bio". **Facebook may NOT** — its Reel description field is guaranteed but external URLs
there are not promised to be clickable. On Facebook use a verified link surface (Page CTA
/ Page bio) plus the memorable short URL **trycurio.app** typed plainly in the
description. Never copy Instagram's "link in bio" wording to Facebook.

**Upload compliance (mandatory while plates are AI-generated):** enable TikTok's
AI-generated-content label and complete YouTube's altered/synthetic-content disclosure.
Both platforms state disclosure does not reduce distribution.

**Upload the ORIGINAL master natively to every platform.** The same file hash may be
reused across platforms with no re-export. **Never download a watermarked platform copy
for reposting.**

Track: profile visits / 1k reached · bio-link clicks / 1k · app-store page views ·
installs · first open · meaningful activation · organic install rate.

## MEASUREMENT PLAN — caption-only vs integrated CTA

**Do not install the in-video CTA on every Reel immediately.** Run a controlled
**10–12 post test**.

- **Group A:** caption + pinned-comment CTA only.
- **Group B:** same caption system **plus** the 0.6–0.9s "GO DEEPER WITH CURIO" overlay.

Hold stable: posting window · video length · topic strength · hook style · caption
density · CTA wording · platform · crossposting settings.

**Primary conversion metrics** (per 1,000 reached): profile visits · link clicks ·
installs · activated users.
**Retention guardrails:** 3s hold · avg watch time · completion · replays · shares ·
saves · skip rate.

**Provisional decision rule:** KEEP the integrated video CTA if it produces a meaningful
conversion lift **while reducing completion or average watch ratio by no more than ~5%**.
If retention declines **without** a clear increase in profile visits, clicks or installs,
**revert to caption-only**.

**Do not optimise for views alone.** A Reel with slightly fewer views but materially more
qualified Curio installs is the better advertisement.

## Standing constraints

1. The signature is **independent of the generation engine** — it is a post-production
   overlay, never baked into scripting/narration/plate generation.
2. Add it **only after the payoff** and **only when loop-safe**.
3. **NEVER retrofit this system into an already-approved master without a new explicit
   review.** (BRINE-POOL v7 is locked; it gets caption-layer conversion only.)
