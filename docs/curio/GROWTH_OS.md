### Elite topic gate (Leon, 2026-07-22)

The discovery dashboard is a recommendation surface, not an inventory of everything
research found. It may show **zero** ideas. A high blended score, a viral source video,
or an easy production cannot rescue a merely educational premise.

Every visible idea must independently clear all of these non-compensable gates:

- the complete contradiction is instantly understandable at frame zero;
- exceptional story power and genuine narrative tension;
- a surprising reveal that deepens the premise rather than merely explaining it;
- a final fact a viewer would immediately tell another person;
- authoritative factual support plus current demand evidence;
- visual proof and a credible rights-safe production route.

The code-enforced floor is 90/100, with hard minimums per dimension. Research that
misses any minimum remains in the intelligence snapshot for audit but is screened out
of the dashboard and cannot be selected. This is an editorial/product rule, not a
claim that a topic will perform; published platform-separated analytics still decide
which mechanisms work.

## 4. Required video architecture

**Visible contradiction -> escalating evidence -> explanation -> transferable final
fact -> clean loop**

- Genuine 1080x1920 source; clean master, no platform watermarks
- ~15–20 seconds
- **Complete premise readable by 0.25s**
- New information every 1.5–2.5s; one continuous story
- **2–4 words per caption card** where practical; **max 2 lines**
- **Stable centre alignment**; bold mobile-readable type
- **No one-word karaoke sequence**
- No obvious AI anatomy or contact defects
- Caption-free engineered silence
- Final caption clears before the loop
- Locked audio restored after Captions.ai export
- Exact transcript-vs-script verification

The ending must give viewers **something they can repeat to another person.**
Procedural closure is insufficient. (This is the advocacy lever.)

## 5. Platform packaging

**Instagram/Facebook:** one curiosity sentence + one line deepening the mystery without
spoiling it + 3–5 SPECIFIC hashtags. No duplicated `#booktok`, generic `#viral`, or
`#iosapp` on an ocean-science video.
> There are lakes beneath the ocean, with their own shorelines and water dense enough
> to sink. The strangest part is what survives around them.
> #OceanScience #DeepSea #MarineBiology

**TikTok:** searchable topic phrase in the caption; 3–5 relevant hashtags; trends only
when they fit Curio's tone; upload the clean master; never hide the subject behind
vague wording.

**YouTube Shorts:** searchable title <~60 chars combining topic + contradiction; one
sentence description. Judge on **Engaged views**, not raw starts (the newer view count
includes every start/replay).
> The Ocean Has Lakes—and Some Are Deadly

## 6. Posting procedure

**Before:** confirm MP4 + resolution · watch once full size and once at 270x480 ·
verify captions vs script · confirm no premature reveal · confirm loudness and silence ·
check cover frame · verify FB crossposting enabled · **save the exact posted file and
its checksum.**

**After:** stay available 30 minutes · answer genuine comments usefully · no engagement
bait · pin a thoughtful comment · Story-share only if it becomes standard for EVERY
post · don't boost analytics experiments · **never edit, delete or repost while
recommendations are increasing.**

## 7. Analytics checkpoints

Capture **each platform separately** at **2h** (technical/distribution), **24h**
(initial), **72h** (recommendation expansion), **7d** (final verdict).

Three gates: **scroll-stop** (skip rate / early retention) · **retention** (avg watch %,
end retention) · **advocacy** (shares, sends, saves, comments, follows).

**Never combine Facebook and Instagram views into one success number** — different
audiences, view definitions and distribution systems. YouTube: Engaged views + relative
watch time. TikTok: search traffic, avg watch time, completion, shares, favorites.
